Glazed Donuts

This easy homemade glazed donuts recipe will satisfy your sweet cravings. Enjoy these delicious donuts with a cup of coffee or tea!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 cup powdered sugar (for glaze)
  • 2 tbsp milk (for glaze)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract (for glaze)

Method:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  5. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  6. Remove donuts and drain on paper towels.
  7. In a separate bowl, whisk together powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla extract to make the glaze.
  8. Dip each donut into the glaze, ensuring it is fully coated.
  9. Allow the glaze to set before serving.
  10. Enjoy your homemade glazed donuts!
